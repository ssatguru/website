GWT

Quick and dirty Spiro grapher created using GWT (Google Web Toolkit) and HTML 5 CANVAS .

Works only on newer browser

Source https://github.com/ssatguru/SpiroGraph. Warning - work in progress :)

To adjust

  • double click inside to stop
  • click and drag mouse on a spiro circle to adjust radius
  • click and drag mouse on the small spiro circle's draw point to adjust length
  • click the clear button to clear graph
  • click check button to check graph without the spiros
  • double click inside to start again.
